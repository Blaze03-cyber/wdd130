About the Temple

The Aba Nigeria Temple is the 121st operating temple of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church) and the 1st temple completed in Nigeria. A concrete bridge constructed by the Church spans the river, granting access to the temple complex, which includes a stake center and an administration office building with guest rooms.
